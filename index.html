About Me

Graciela (Grace) Diaz is an entrepreneurial professional with a background in Information Technology (IT), specifically in the architecture, design, development, and implementation of enterprise software solutions. She has extensive experience in systems architecture, user interface design, applications development, network administration and support. She is an expert in leveraging technology assets to solve business problems and has excellent analytical and problem-solving skills. Her experience includes providing IT solutions to the Insurance, Financial, Pharmaceutical, and Healthcare industries.

Grace has excellent leadership and communication abilities. She is skilled at presenting IT projects for funding purposes. She is a natural liaison between senior management, clients and technology staff and can speak both business and technical languages with ease.

Grace operates in a matrix environment and works with cross-functional teams. She has managed multiple projects simultaneously and manages a staff of developers, both on and offshore.
